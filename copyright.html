INTRODUCTION
The following Privacy Policy outlines how we obtain and store customers information, also known as ‘Personally identifiable information’ (PII). The information listed in the policy applies to all the information that we collect about you

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
When placing an order or registering on our website, we save multiple pieces of information which includes:
- Your Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number ( not required )
- Demographic Information
- Consensual Records
- Payment Details
We collect personal information from you such as your Name, Email Address, Address and Phone Number to comply with laws and to distinguish you from other users that take advantage of our service. Furthermore, we collect your demographic information to assist us when targeting specific users with advertisement messages. We save your consensual records to prevent sending you emails and other related marketing information when you have opted out. Finally, we store your Payment Details such as your Email in some payments to assist us with identifying users in the event of a payment dispute.

HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?
We will not share your information with any third-party customers without consent beforehand. However, we may share your information with legal authorities such as the Police if you are found to be committing a crime on our service, or if they specifically request your information.

All of your information is stored in secure databases that cannot be accessed by any member of the public, as well as the staff team at Zlorical. Additionally, we encrypt all secure information via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) which all of our systems and our main website have.

Zlorical will never modify your saved data unless specifically requested by you. Furthermore when requesting support for your product/service you have an option to disallow our technical team from accessing your service when opening a ticket.

COOKIES
Cookies are small files that are stored on your computer through your web browser. Certain aspects of our website require cookies in order to function such as our Shopping Cart and your Authentication session keeping you logged in to our panels. We also use cookies to remember your preferences when navigating our website to ensure your future visits follow the same protocol. Finally we use cookies for security reasons.

You can remove your cookies stored on your computer by visiting your website browser settings and removing them.
